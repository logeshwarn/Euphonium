About TCE Scheduler

Our Purpose

TCE Scheduler is designed to streamline academic scheduling and enhance faculty-student interactions at Thiagarajar College of Engineering. Our platform facilitates seamless coordination of consultations, lab sessions, and academic meetings.

Our Mission

To revolutionize academic scheduling by providing an efficient, user-friendly platform that promotes better communication between faculty and students, ultimately enhancing the learning experience at TCE.

Key Features

  • Easy scheduling of academic consultations and meetings
  • Seamless faculty-student communication
  • Real-time notifications for schedule updates
  • Efficient time management tools
